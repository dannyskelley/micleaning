Why Pet Waste Cleanup Matters

Pet waste contains bacteria like E. coli and salmonella that can stay in the soil for years. Regular cleanup prevents odors, protects your lawn, reduces pest attraction and helps keep your family and pets safe. It also helps maintain property value and improves the overall appearance of your yard.
